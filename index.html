What am I about?

My name is Lawrence Hoerst. I grew up in Harrison, Ohio, and I'm currently a senior majoring in Computer Science and Mathematics at Berea College in Berea, Kentucky. From the moment I arrived at Berea, I knew I was in the right place. Not only was I awarded a full-tuition scholarship, but I also gained the opportunity to learn from some of the best instructors in my fields. At college, I dove deeply into both subjects, exploring topics such as networking, graph theory, combinatorics, linear algebra, and operating systems, among others. I've dedicated myself to both rigorous academic pursuits and taking on leadership roles in campus organizations.

Full-Stack Developer and AI Researcher

During the summer of 2024 I was fortunate enough to partner with Next Generation Justice and work as a full-stack web developer and AI research assistant. As interns, my team and I strived to make the law more approachable for the common person by leveraging AI technologies. During the course of this internship I collaborated with two fellow interns in order to fine-tune pretrained AI models to speak legalese and create a consumer-facing React app with Next.js.

Full-Stack Developer

During the summer of 2023, I interned with Berea College's software development team, working with 9 other interns to enhance features that saved volunteer time and money. CELTS, focusing on community service and civic engagement, saw an annual savings of $60,000 from our custom event and volunteer management solution. I streamlined the volunteer process, enabling returning volunteers to store emergency contact and insurance details, reducing repetitive form-filling. I currently work here part time.

IT Student Manager

I became skilled in Windows OS and debugging issues. I also reduced lost machines by implementing new tracking systems, improving accountability and efficiency.
